The New Generation of Claims Managmet

New and innovate treatment options are constatly being introduced into the medical field as a result of rigorous medical research and rapid technological advance.
The abundance of options complicates achieving quality medical care due to the difficuly of sifting
through the slew of available treatments.
This often results in the prescrition of unnecessary treatments and
procedures either due to a lack of available information or by succumbing to finencial considerations.

Claims Managment: Not Just Eligibility

Within this medical environment, policy caps and exclusions are not sufficient to meet these challenges. Generic eligibility
definitions are either over inclusive; leading to uncontrolled spending, or under inclusive, damaging the patient's treatment
and lowering customer satisfaction.

Applying quality medical standards and an individual review to the claims process serves as an effective middle ground
between these two polarities. Medical review excludes treatments that are deemed ineffective or irrelevant, while suitable
options would be included. In this way excessive spending is avoided while ensuring a high level of medical care and
customer satisfaction.

New Comprehensive Medical Claims Managment

Each medical review carried out by MedixCorp consist of four main components.

Medical Eligibility

Pertains to the review of medical policy definitions such as chronic,
cosmetic or experimental. These definitions are constantly changing
and often unclear. Applying them to a specific claim requires medical
expertise and a deep understating of the patient's condition.
Preforming this check is an essential part of the Medix claims review.

Medical Validity

Ensures that the proposed treatment is fact recognized as a valid option for the patient's diagnosis and condition. Our physicians assess each claim and apply to it experience, expertise and international recognized treatments guidelines.

Medical Necessity

Includes assessing whether or not a valid treatment is necessary
give the patient's individual situation. As with validity, international
guidelines are applied as well as specialist expertise and experience.

Length of Stay Managment

Our necessity and validity review applies to specific treatments and
inpatients stay alike. Through this method , we are able to strike an
optimal balance, keeping the patient admitted while required, and
discharging immediately thereafter in order to prevent
further complications.

Excessive spending is avoided while ensuring a high level of medical care and customer satisfaction.
